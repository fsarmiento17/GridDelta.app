GridDelta Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

GridDelta does not require account creation and does not collect personal data directly from users.

Data Sources

The app displays Formula 1 results and analytics using third-party motorsport data APIs. Requests may be processed by those providers according to their policies.

Data We Store On Device

GridDelta stores cached race and analytics data locally on your device to improve performance and offline behavior. This data is not used to identify you.

Tracking and Ads

GridDelta does not use advertising SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

GridDelta is a general audience app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
